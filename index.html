who we are

Our objective is to provide a UAS that can be modified as per its application, while the air frame and other critical components remain the same. The UAS will be able to complete multiple tasks in a single flight (if required), or it will be capable of completing tasks of different nature without much hardware changes. We have categorized our product according to its various applications.

Surveillance

Capable of providing real time infrastructural surveillance for public sector companies like oil and natural gas, power, railways etc.

Security

Can be useful for Homeland security forces, police forces to create a "law enforcement response ecosystem".

Military

The UAS will be designed to provide video inputs, carry payloads of importance, capable of being armed (UCAV), and be stealthy. It will be able to take off/land on land as well as water.

Civil Uses

The UAS for civil purposees will be designed to handle tasks like search & rescue operations, payload delivery by e-retailers, ferrying critical drugs or organs for transplant, traffic monitoring, checking illegal activities, agricultural purposes etc.
